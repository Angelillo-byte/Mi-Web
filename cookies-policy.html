What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. These files allow us to remember your preferences and improve your experience on the site.

Types of cookies we use

  • Technical cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and allow access to secure areas.
  • Analytical cookies: These help us understand how users interact with our website, improving its functionality.

Managing cookies

You can configure your browser to accept, block, or delete cookies. Please note that some features of the website may not function correctly if you disable cookies.
